Age confirmation on the Web is broken.

To check the age of a user, many sites simply have the user input their age. However, the user could simply claim they are N years old and the website wouldn't know.

Another solution could be to require identifiable information, but then that necessitates security.

So I've come up with a simple fix to figure out the age of a user:

  1. Click on the link for the age N years you want to verify (Ideally you do this the moment you are born)
  2. Keep the page open in your browser for N years
  3. After N years, the page will show that you are verified as being at least N years old!
  4. Then take a screenshot of the page and you can show it as proof of age for websites, bars, etc.
